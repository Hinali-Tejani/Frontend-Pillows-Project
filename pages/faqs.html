Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pillow Lab?

Pillow Lab is a premium retailer specializing in high-quality pillows designed for ultimate comfort and support.

What types of pillows do you offer?

We offer a variety of pillows, including luxury, comfort, and eco-friendly options to suit different preferences and needs.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by visiting the Contact Us page and filling out the form.

Do you offer a guarantee on your products?

Yes, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all our products. If you're not happy with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on all orders within the continental United States. International shipping rates vary based on location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.
